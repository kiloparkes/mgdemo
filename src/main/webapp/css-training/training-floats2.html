Finally, floating every column also avoids a 3-pixel bug that affects Internet Explorer 6 and earlier (see page 335).

In addition, floating more than just the sidebars lets you change the order of your divs in the HTML.

Without the abality to change the oder of HTML elements in the page, the main content will always need to be after the less important sidebars. This is bad idea for search engines, and for devices that reads the page for the visually impaired since on every page the have to read the sidebar info before they get to the content.
